Setext Documents Mirror

Here are local copies of some Setext documents, made available to document the prehistory of the Docutils project (and especially the reStructuredText markup).

The source for the original Setext (structure-enhanced text) documents was http://www.bsdi.com/setext/, but it seems to have disappeared from the Web. The files in the "Setext mirror" are all the files relating to Setext that I have (including a tarball of the lot).

I have not been able to locate the originators of Setext, Ian Feldman or Tony Sanders. If you know how to contact them, or if you know of an official repository for the Setext documents, please inform me.

David Goodger, 2002-03-25
